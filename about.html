About Opteo

Made for Adwords people, by Adwords people.

We built Opteo because we needed a way to manage all of our Adwords clients in one place. None of the available solutions could come to terms with the complexity of our approach to Adwords. So we made one that could. More than ever, we're committed to improving the day to day experience of Adwords people around the world.

Helping to build a better Adwords (for everyone).

Forget the days of tireless excel analysis, poring over manual changes and creating useless reports. We're building towards a future where nurturing client relationships, refining long term strategies and engaging your creative side will make the difference between "just another" campaign and a world-beating one.
